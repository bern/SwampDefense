SWAMP DEFENSE
an Android application

"Welcome to the Swamp, where only GATORS get out alive!" Swamp Defense is an Android application where you take the help as Albert, defending the Swamp from other mascots in the SEC (and a particularly pesky ACC mascot). See how long you can last as you chomp, growl, and turn all who oppose you into GATOR BAIT!
